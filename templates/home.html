Initiative

What Motivates Us ?

The local water resources were sufficient for people in the early days. But nowadays additional requirement is becoming necessary due to population explosion and catchment area. Though the amount of rainfall every year is almost same, it's affecting the livelihood of people and nature.

What is the Objective ?

Create awareness among the people about the need of storing of water. To showcase the use of modern tools like Machine Learning in solving society related problems.To predict whether the rainwater collected in that ponds is sufficient or not.

What are the expecting outcomes ?

Our website predicts whether the amount of water stored in pond is Deficit or not by taking consideration of many factors like rainfall, percolation of water to ground,evaporation of water and the current population of that place..
